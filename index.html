Travel to Florence

Significance

Florence a city in central Italy and the capital city of the Tuscany region.

The city attracts millions of tourists each year, and UNESCO declared the Historic Centre of Florence a World Heritage Site in 1982.

Florence is well known for:

  • Churches
  • Renaissance art and architecture
  • Monuments

The city also contains

  • Numerous museums
  • Art galleries

    • Must visited art galleries:

    1. Uffizi Gallery

    2. Palazzo Pitti

International recognitions

Because of it's artistic and architectural heritage, Forbes has ranked it as one of the most beautiful cities in the world. and plays an important role in Italian fashion, and is also ranked in the top 15 fashion capitals of the world by Global Language Monitor.

Activities

  • Food and Wine

    • Great food and excellent wine in not difficult to find in Florence. If you are fond of wine, dont leave Florence with out doing wine testing.

  • Shopping

    • Shopping in Florence offers so much from fashion clothing to stunning antiques, leather handcrafts and glittery gold.Some of the favorites include leather jackets, bags, shoes, belts and gloves, stunning marble mosaics, beautiful necklaces, earrings & charms and cutting edge fashion clothing for men, women and children.

  • Cooking Classes

    • Is cooking your hobby or you want to learn new traditions? learning how to make pasta or traditional Tuscan dishes such as ribollita, panzanella, wild boar sauce or making pici! Lots of options, from half day or one day classes or to multiple days or even a week devoted to the culinary arts!

  • Studying

    • Italian language school Leonardo Da Vinci, Polimoda fashion and marketing school are Well known schools in Florence:

Best times to visit Florence

Florence is the best place to visit any time in the year, there are different activities and events happen in all seasons but Early summer and fall are best times to visit Florence.
